We Are Uplifting Lives

Uplifting Lives One Child a time!

Uplifting Lives is an NPO that aids in feeding,educating and housing hungry,illiterate and abandoned children in South Africa.
We are registered under the South African Non-Profit Companies Act of 2008. Uplifting Lives is the the future of changing South African childrens lives.



We are here to help put South Africa on the map by changing the lives of the youth because here at Uplifting Lives we believe that they are the future. Love reigns in this environment we are building because through love, we know that we can completely transform the lives of millions in SA. As we embark on this journey of greatness we belive that we need to unite as a country for a better tomorrow.

Tens of thousands of volunteers, donors and supporters advance the Make-A-Wish® vision to grant the wish of every child diagnosed with a critical illness. In the U.S. and its territories, a wish is granted every 34 minutes. A wish can be that spark that helps these children believe that anything is possible and gives them the strength to fight harder against their illnesses. This one belief guides us and inspires us to grant wishes that change the lives of the kids we serve.



For children diagnosed with critical illnesses, a wish come true can be a crucial turning point in their lives. A wish can be that spark that helps these children believe that anything is possible and gives them the strength to fight harder against their illnesses. This impact is why we are driven to make every one of these wishes come true.



The Make-A-Wish mission is too inspiring for just one country. Today, Make-A-Wish International is the hub for wish-granting activities in nearly 50 countries, outside of the United States, on five continents and counting. Affiliates in each country bring the mission to life in communities around the globe.
